School Notes

Goal of this application is to help teachers keep track of all the students, their assignments and lessons. In future updates we will allow all the teachers to share school data, allowing each teacher to see each individual student's progress.

Implemented:

  • Meetings: Summary of previous meetings and school assignments
  • Students: Simple list keeping track of all the students
  • About: Application introduction, and feature summary
